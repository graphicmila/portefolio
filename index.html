About Me

I’m someone who truly believes in the power of learning to transform lives. Whether it's designing engaging e-learning experiences or delivering training that makes a real difference, I’m passionate about creating opportunities for growth. I thrive on bringing new ideas to life and helping others develop skills that will shape their future.

With a background as a Learning Specialist and a love for all things creative, I focus on building learning experiences that are both effective and enjoyable. I love the challenge of taking complex information and making it accessible, relatable, and impactful.

What I do:
I’m currently an Instructional Designer at Foundever, where I develop e-learning content and training plans, manage learning calendars, and help facilitate growth within the team.

Beyond work:
When I’m not creating learning experiences, you can find me exploring my passion for literature, illustration, and technology. I’m always looking for ways to blend creativity with education.
